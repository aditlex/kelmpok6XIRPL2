About DPIB

"Building Design and
Construction - Shaping spaces,
building futures."

Building Modeling and Drawing Techniques: Learn how to make building engineering drawings manually or digitally with CAD (Computer-Aided Design) and BIM (Building Information Modeling) software.

Construction and Building Structures: Understand the basic concepts of building structures such as foundations, walls, roofs, and other construction techniques that affect the strength and stability of buildings.

Material and Cost Calculation: Understand how to calculate the material requirements and construction costs to build a project. It helps students prepare budgets and understand the tender process in the building industry.
